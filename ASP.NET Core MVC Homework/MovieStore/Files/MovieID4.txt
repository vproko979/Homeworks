"Sicario"

Sicario ([si.ˈka.ɾjo], Spanish for "Hitman") is a 2015 American crime thriller film directed by Denis Villeneuve, 
written by Taylor Sheridan and starring Emily Blunt, Benicio del Toro, Josh Brolin, and Victor Garber. 
The film follows a principled FBI agent who is enlisted by a government task force to bring down the leader 
of a powerful and brutal Mexican drug cartel.
